# Business Case

Bright Sparks Ltd needed a better way to manage inventory and sales information. Manual tracking can lead to duplicated data, inaccurate stock levels and slow reporting.

A relational database was the right solution because the business data is connected. Products link to suppliers, inventory links to products, and sales link to sold products.

The database improves accuracy, makes reporting easier and gives the business clearer information about stock levels and product sales.
