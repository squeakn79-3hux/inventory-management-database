# Requirements Analysis

## Functional Requirements

The system needed to:

- Store product details including name, description, price and category.
- Store supplier details including name, address and contact number.
- Track inventory levels, quantities sold and stock on order.
- Record sales transactions.
- Generate a weekly low-stock query.
- Produce a top-selling products report.
- Provide forms for adding, updating, deleting and searching records.

## Non-Functional Requirements

The system also needed to be:

- Accurate.
- Easy to use.
- Secure through role-based permissions.
- Able to support basic backup and recovery.
- Reliable enough for small business use.
