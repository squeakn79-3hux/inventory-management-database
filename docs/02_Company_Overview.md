# Company Overview

**Bright Sparks Ltd** is a fictional Auckland-based electronics retailer. The business sells electronic products and manages supplier, product, inventory and sales information.

As the business grows, manual record keeping becomes harder to manage. A database gives the business a cleaner way to store information, track stock and produce reports.

This company scenario gave the project a realistic purpose instead of being built as a database exercise only.
